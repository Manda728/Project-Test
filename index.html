Equine Massage

Why is massage beneficial for the horse?

"Equine sports massage is the therapeutic application of massage techniques applied to the horse by the manipulation of the soft tissues to help achieve increased performance levels and endurance, treat or prevent injury and improve recovery rates"

Equine massage is not only good for the horse's overall well-being but may also benefit the horse by:

  • Improving muscle tone
  • Reduce adhesions
  • Help the muscles to work at maximum efficiency
  • Increase blood circulation
  • Increase the flow of lymph
